Product Case Studies

Workforce Management (symplr)

The Why

WFM's core goal is having the right nurse at the right place at the right time. However, last-minute open shifts were extremely difficult to fill, directly impacting patient care.

The How

Orchestrated cross-functional alignment (Engineering, UX, Support, Services) to completely reimagine how open shifts were promoted to nurses by introducing "reverse auctions."

The Win

Dramatically improved last-minute shift fill rates and reduced staffer scheduling effort, ensuring optimal patient care coverage while significantly lowering premium staffing costs for hospital networks.

Midas Care Management (symplr)

The Why

The core goal of Midas is to proactively manage clinical risk and patient safety. However, hospitals struggled with fragmented data, manual incident reporting, and complex compliance audits.

The How

Orchestrated a cohesive multi-product strategy to streamline electronic workflows for case management, incident reporting, and real-time quality analytics.

The Win

Empowered healthcare organizations to seamlessly track adverse events and ensure Joint Commission audit readiness, fundamentally driving safer, data-driven patient outcomes.

Enterprise Ecosystem Integration (symplr)

The Why

The How

Championed a multi-product interoperability execution, collaborating seamlessly with internal stakeholders across engineering, UX, and architecture to build unified standards.

The Win

Successfully delivered multiple cross-product integrations, breaking down operational silos, drastically improving user experience, and driving measurable customer value across the portfolio.
